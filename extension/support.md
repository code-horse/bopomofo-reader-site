# Bopomofo Reader Extension - Support

## About Bopomofo Reader Extension

Bopomofo Reader is a Chrome browser extension that adds bopomofo annotations to Chinese characters on any web page. It features an enhanced bopomofo-pro engine with modern Chinese dictionary and 200+ polyphonic word corrections, whole-page bopomofo mode, hover tooltips, text-to-speech, selection speech, and supports 38 interface languages.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**  
A: No. Bopomofo Reader Extension works entirely locally and does not require any account registration.

**Q: Is my data stored on a server?**  
A: No. All your settings are stored locally in your browser. We do not operate any servers.

**Q: Does the extension contain ads?**  
A: No. Bopomofo Reader Extension is ad-free.

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**  
A: Bopomofo Reader Extension is designed for Google Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, etc.).

### Bopomofo & Annotations

**Q: How do I enable bopomofo annotations?**  
A: Click the Bopomofo Reader icon in your browser toolbar, then toggle "Enable Annotator" on. You can also click the floating button on the bottom-right of any page to toggle whole-page bopomofo mode.

**Q: Why are some characters annotated incorrectly?**  
A: Chinese has many polyphonic characters (多音字). Our enhanced engine corrects 200+ common cases, but some rare combinations may still be inaccurate. Please report specific cases to help us improve.

**Q: Can I choose between tone marks and no tones?**  
A: Yes. Open the extension popup and change "Bopomofo Style" to switch between "With Tone Marks" (pīn yīn) and "Without Tones" (pin yin).

**Q: Why doesn't it work on some pages?**  
A: For security reasons, browser extensions cannot run on special pages like `chrome://`, `edge://`, browser settings, or the Chrome Web Store.

### Text-to-Speech

**Q: How do I hear the pronunciation of a character?**  
A: Hover over an annotated character to see the tooltip, then click the speaker button. In whole-page bopomofo mode, you can also click directly on the bopomofo text above any character.

**Q: How do I read aloud a sentence?**  
A: Select any Chinese text on the page. A floating speaker button will appear — click it to read aloud. You can also right-click and choose "Speak Selection".

**Q: Can I adjust the speech rate?**  
A: Yes. The "Sentence Speech Rate" setting in the popup controls the speed for sentence reading. Single character pronunciation always uses the default rate for clarity.

**Q: No sound from text-to-speech?**  
A: Please check your system volume. TTS quality depends on your operating system's Chinese voice support. On some systems, you may need to install a Chinese voice pack.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The extension icon is grayed out.**  
A: This usually means you're on a page where extensions cannot run (e.g., `chrome://` pages). Navigate to a regular web page.

**Q: Annotations are not showing after enabling.**  
A: Try clicking the "Annotate" button in the popup, or refresh the page. If the page was loaded before the extension was enabled, it may need a refresh.
